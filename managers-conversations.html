Conversations with Your Manager

Start strong with these 5 key conversations to build trust, get feedback, and grow with intention.

Whether it’s your first week or your tenth, these conversation starters help align your work with your goals.

🗣️ 5 Topics to Discuss

  • Setting expectations: “What does success look like in my first 90 days?”
  • Getting feedback early: “How will I know how I’m doing?”
  • Clarifying communication: “What’s your preferred way to stay in touch?”
  • Exploring growth paths: “What skills should I build in the next 6 months?”
  • Sharing what energizes you: “What parts of my job can I lean into more?”
