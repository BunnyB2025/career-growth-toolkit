Skill Jumpstart Series

Quick, practical support for your first 90 days on the job

What is the Jumpstart Series?

These short, mobile-friendly courses are designed to help you feel confident and supported as you get started. Each one is under 45 minutes and focuses on practical skills you can use right away — communication, clarity, and staying grounded under pressure.

Why It Matters for You

  • Reduces first-90-day stress and uncertainty
  • Builds confidence in communication and collaboration
  • Helps you manage emotions and focus on what matters most

Jumpstart is here to remind you: You don’t have to know everything on day one. You just have to begin.

  • Communicating with Confidence
  • Letting Go of Overthinking
  • Building Resilience

  • Improving Your Listening Skills
  • Managing Your Emotions at Work
